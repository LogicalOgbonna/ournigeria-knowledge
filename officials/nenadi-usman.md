# Nenadi Usman

> elected

## Biography

Senator Nenadi Esther Usman, a former Minister of Finance (2006-2007) and former Senator for Kaduna South (2011-2015), was elected substantive National Chairman of the Labour Party at its 28 April 2026 convention in Umuahia, having led the caretaker committee from September 2024. The Court of Appeal affirmed her leadership in April 2026; the Julius Abure faction continues to contest it.
