# Yabagi Sani

> elected

## Biography

Engr. Yabagi Yusuf Sani is the National Chairman of the Action Democratic Party (ADP) and was the party's presidential candidate in the 2019 and 2023 general elections. An energy expert from Bida, Niger State, he also served as National Chairman of IPAC from 2021 until December 2023.
