# Bishop Amakiri

> elected

## Biography

Comrade Bishop Emmanuel Amakiri is the founding National Chairman of the Young Progressives Party, a position he has held since the party's registration in 2017 and to which he was re-elected for a second term in 2023; he remained chairman as of June 2026.
