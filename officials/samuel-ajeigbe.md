# Samuel Ajeigbe

> elected

## Biography

Pastor Samuel (Sam) Ajeigbe became AAC National Chairman on 26 May 2026 after Omoyele Sowore stepped down upon securing the party's 2027 presidential ticket. He had previously served as the party's Deputy National Chairman (Admin) on the NWC elected at the November 2025 Abuja convention.
