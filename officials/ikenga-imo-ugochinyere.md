# Ikenga Imo Ugochinyere

> elected

## Biography

Hon. Ikenga Imo Ugochinyere is the National Leader of the Action Peoples Party (APP) and the member of the House of Representatives for Ideato North/Ideato South Federal Constituency of Imo State. He is the party's most prominent public figure.
