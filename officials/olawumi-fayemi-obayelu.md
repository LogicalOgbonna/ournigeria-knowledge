# Olawumi Fayemi-Obayelu

> mha (APC) · [APC](/parties/apc.md)

## Biography

Born March 20, 1986 in Lagos State. Member of the Akenneyin royal dynasty. Chairs the House Committee on Women Affairs. Received Outstanding Gender Equity Crusader of the Year award from Trumpet Magazine in 2025.

## Political Career

- **mha**, 2023–present, [APC](/parties/apc.md)
