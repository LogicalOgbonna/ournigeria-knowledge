# Emmanuel Nwachukwu

> senator (APGA) · [APGA](/parties/apga.md)

## Biography

Senator representing Anambra South in the 10th National Assembly. Won by-election on August 16, 2025 to replace the late Ifeanyi Patrick Ubah (YPP) who died July 27, 2024. Sworn in October 8, 2025.

- **senator**, 2023–present, [APGA](/parties/apga.md)
