# Abdulrahman Mohammed

> elected

## Biography

Abdulrahman Mohammed, from Nasarawa State, is the INEC-recognised National Chairman of the PDP (Wike-backed faction), confirmed on INEC's portal on 30 March 2026 after the 29 March 2026 Abuja convention, having previously served as Acting National Chairman. His chairmanship is heavily contested amid the party's 2025-2026 leadership crisis.
