# Amos Yohanna

> senator (APC) · [APC](/parties/apc.md)

## Biography

Politician representing Adamawa North in the 10th National Assembly. Replaced Ishaku Abbo after the Court of Appeal sacked Abbo on October 16, 2023 for electoral violation. Originally elected on PDP platform, defected to APC in March 2026.

## Political Career

- **senator**, 2023–present, [APC](/parties/apc.md)
