# Dan Nwanyanwu

> elected

## Biography

Chief Barr. Dan Nwanyanwu is the National Chairman of the Zenith Labour Party, re-elected to a four-year term at the party's February 2025 National Convention. He emerged as ZLP's consensus presidential candidate for the 2027 general election at a Special Convention in Abuja on 30 May 2026.
