# Bala Yunusa Mohammed

> elected

## Biography

A Kaduna State-born politician and former National Organising Secretary of the NNPP, installed by the party's National Working Committee in April 2026 after former chairman Ajuji Ahmed defected to the ADC. His chairmanship is contested: an FCT High Court (November 2025) affirmed a rival, Dr. Agbo Gilbert Major, leaving the leadership unresolved.
