# Vernimbe A. James

> elected

## Biography

National Secretary of the Kenneth Udeze-led faction of Action Alliance; co-signed the party's correspondence to INEC and had his tenure extended at the party's February 2025 national convention. His standing is faction-dependent given AA's unresolved leadership dispute.
