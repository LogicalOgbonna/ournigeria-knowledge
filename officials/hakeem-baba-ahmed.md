# Hakeem Baba-Ahmed

> elected

## Biography

A retired Nigerian civil servant who rose to Federal Permanent Secretary and served as Secretary of INEC, later Special Adviser (Political) to Vice-President Kashim Shettima before resigning in 2025. He was elected PRP National Chairman at the party's 28 March 2026 Abuja convention and recognised by INEC on 1 May 2026, though a rival faction has petitioned INEC disputing it.
