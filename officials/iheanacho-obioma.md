# Iheanacho Obioma

> elected

## Biography

Hon. Iheanacho Obioma was elected National Secretary of the Labour Party at the party's 28 April 2026 national convention in Umuahia, replacing Senator Darlington Nwokocha. A former member of the House of Representatives (Ikwuano/Umuahia, 1999-2003), he served as DG of the 2023 campaign that elected Abia Governor Alex Otti.
