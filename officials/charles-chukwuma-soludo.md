# Charles Chukwuma Soludo

> elected

## Biography

Governor of Anambra State and a former Central Bank of Nigeria governor, Charles Chukwuma Soludo was appointed National Leader of APGA by the party's NEC on 13 January 2023. His standing as de-facto party leader was reinforced by his landslide re-election as Anambra governor on 8 November 2025.

## Elections

- **2025 gubernatorial** — [APGA](/parties/apga.md) — won [^e1]
