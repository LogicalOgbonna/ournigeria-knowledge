# Lawal Samusideen Adekunle

> mha (APC) · [APC](/parties/apc.md)

## Biography

Popularly called 'Hon. Lawee'. Won Ogun Waterside seat on PDP ticket 18 March 2023; sworn in 20 June 2023. Chairman, House Committee on Environment; Vice-Chairman, Lands and Housing.
