# Nentawe Goshwe Yilwatda

> elected

## Biography

Nentawe Yilwatda is a Nigerian engineer, professor of electrical engineering and politician who became APC National Chairman on 24 July 2025 and was re-elected at the party's 8th national convention on 28 March 2026. He previously served as Minister of Humanitarian Affairs and Poverty Reduction (2024-2025) and was the APC governorship candidate in Plateau State in 2023.
