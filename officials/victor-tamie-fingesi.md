# Victor Tamie Fingesi

> elected

## Biography

Victor Tamie Fingesi is the National Secretary of the Action Democratic Party (ADP). He previously contested as the ADP governorship candidate in Rivers State in the 2023 elections and frequently issues the party's official statements.
