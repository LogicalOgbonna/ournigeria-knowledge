# Adebukola Ajaja

> elected

## Biography

Hon. Dr. Adebukola Abiola Ajaja is a Nigerian politician from Oyo State who represented the Ibadan North-East/Ibadan South-East federal constituency in the House of Representatives from 2011 to 2015. She serves as National Secretary of the Accord Party within the INEC-recognised NWC.
