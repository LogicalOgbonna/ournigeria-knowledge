# Charles Soludo

> governor (APGA) · [APGA](/parties/apga.md) · [Anambra](/states/anambra.md)

## Biography

Professor and former Central Bank of Nigeria Governor (2004-2009) serving as Governor of Anambra State since 2022. Visiting scholar at IMF, Cambridge, Brookings Institution, and Oxford.
