# David Mark

> elected

## Biography

David Alechenu Bonaventure Mark is a retired Nigerian Army brigadier general who served as President of the Nigerian Senate from 2007 to 2015, the longest-serving in the nation's history. In July 2025 he was named Interim National Chairman of the ADC after the opposition coalition adopted the party; his chairmanship was affirmed by the Supreme Court on 30 April 2026.
