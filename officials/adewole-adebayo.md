# Adewole Adebayo

> elected

## Biography

Prince Adewole Adebayo is a Nigerian lawyer, international arbitrator and media entrepreneur (founder of KAFTAN TV) who is the de-facto national leader of the SDP. He was the party's 2023 presidential candidate and was returned unopposed as its 2027 presidential flagbearer at the May 2026 convention in Bauchi.
