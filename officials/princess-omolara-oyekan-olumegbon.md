# Princess Omolara Oyekan-Olumegbon

> mha (APC) · [APC](/parties/apc.md)

## Biography

Princess Omolara Omotade Oyekan-Olumegbon is the daughter of the late Oba Adeyinka Oyekan II, Oba of Lagos (1965-2003). She chairs the House Committee on Women Affairs, Poverty Alleviation, and Job Creation.

## Political Career

- **mha**, 2023–present, [APC](/parties/apc.md)
