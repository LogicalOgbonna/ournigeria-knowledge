# Olu Agunloye

> elected

## Biography

A former Minister of State for Defence (Navy) and former Minister of Power and Steel, Olu Agunloye serves as SDP National Secretary, returned to the post at the party's May 2026 national convention. His tenure was challenged by a rival faction but he was retained under the INEC-recognised convention slate.
