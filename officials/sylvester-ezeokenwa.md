# Sylvester Ezeokenwa

> elected

## Biography

A legal practitioner and former personal assistant to Anambra Governor Chukwuma Soludo, Sylvester 'Sly' Ezeokenwa was elected APGA National Chairman at the party's Awka convention in May 2023. His chairmanship was affirmed by the Supreme Court on 27 November 2024 and restored by INEC in December 2024.
