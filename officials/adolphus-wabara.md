# Adolphus Wabara

> elected

## Biography

Senator Adolphus Nduneweh Wabara, 10th President of the Nigerian Senate (2003-2005), is Chairman of the PDP Board of Trustees. Following the Supreme Court ruling of 30 April 2026 that voided the contested conventions, the Wabara-led BoT assumed administrative leadership of the party, positioning him as de-facto party leader amid the leadership vacuum.
