# Rauf Aregbesola

> elected

## Biography

Ogbeni Rauf Adesoji Aregbesola served as Governor of Osun State from 2010 to 2018 and as Minister of Interior from 2019 to 2023. In July 2025 he accepted appointment as Interim National Secretary of the ADC under the opposition coalition, a role reaffirmed following the Supreme Court ruling of 30 April 2026.
