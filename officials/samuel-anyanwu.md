# Samuel Anyanwu

> elected

## Biography

Senator Samuel Anyanwu, a former senator for Imo East (2015-2019), is listed by INEC (March 2026) as PDP National Secretary within the Wike-backed NWC. His tenure is sharply disputed: he was expelled for 'anti-party activities' in November 2025, and a rival camp recognises Setonji Koshoedo as Acting National Secretary.
