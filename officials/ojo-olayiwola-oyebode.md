# Ojo Olayiwola Oyebode

> mha (APC) · [APC](/parties/apc.md)

## Biography

Hon. Ojo Olayiwola Oyebode was born on April 4, 1975, in Ipee, Oyun Local Government Area, Kwara State. He began his political career as an Ipee Ward Councilor (2007-2010) and later served as Vice-Chairman of Oyun Local Government. He was first elected to the Kwara State House of Assembly in 2019 and re-elected in 2023, representing Oke-Ogun constituency. He was elected Deputy Speaker of the 10th Assembly on its inauguration.
