# Adewale Olumide Egbedun

> mha (Accord) · [Accord](/parties/accord.md)

## Biography

Adewale Olumide Egbedun was born on 29 June 1985 in Odo-Otin, Osun State. He was elected to the Osun State House of Assembly in 2023 on the PDP platform representing Odo-Otin constituency and was elected Speaker of the 8th Assembly on 6 June 2023, becoming one of the youngest assembly speakers in Osun history. In February 2026 he led the mass defection of 25 assembly members to the Accord Party.
