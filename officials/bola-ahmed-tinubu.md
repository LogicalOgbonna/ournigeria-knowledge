# Bola Ahmed Tinubu

> elected

## Biography

President Bola Ahmed Tinubu is the de-facto national leader of the APC, presiding over the party's 2026 national convention. He is President of Nigeria (since 2023) and a former two-term Governor of Lagos State (1999-2007).
