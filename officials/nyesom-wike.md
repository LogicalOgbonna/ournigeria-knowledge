# Nyesom Wike

> governor (PDP) · [PDP](/parties/pdp.md) · [FCT](/states/fct.md)

## Biography

Lawyer and politician serving as Minister of the Federal Capital Territory since 2023. Former Governor of Rivers State (2015-2023). Previously served as Minister of State for Education and Federal Minister of Education under President Goodluck Jonathan.

- **governor**, [FCT](/states/fct.md), 2023–present, [PDP](/parties/pdp.md)
