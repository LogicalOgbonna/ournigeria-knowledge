# Surajudeen Ajibola Basiru

> elected

## Biography

Senator Dr. Surajudeen Ajibola Basiru is a Nigerian lawyer who has served as APC National Secretary since August 2023 and was returned to the office at the 28 March 2026 national convention. He represented Osun Central Senatorial District in the Senate (2019-2023), where he was Senate Spokesperson.
