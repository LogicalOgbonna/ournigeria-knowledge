# Abdulmalik Mohammed Sarkindaji

> mha (APC) · [APC](/parties/apc.md)

## Biography

Nigerian lawyer and public servant. Served as Executive Chairman of Mariga LGA (2016), Commissioner for Local Government and Chieftaincy Affairs (2019–2021), Commissioner for Youth and Sport (2021–2023). Elected to Niger State House of Assembly in 2023 and unanimously elected Speaker on June 13, 2023.
