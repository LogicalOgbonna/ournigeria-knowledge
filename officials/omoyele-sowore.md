# Omoyele Sowore

> elected

## Biography

Omoyele Sowore is the founder and de-facto national leader of the AAC and the publisher of Sahara Reporters. He served as AAC National Chairman (re-elected November 2025) until stepping down on 26 May 2026 after being adopted as the party's 2027 presidential candidate.
