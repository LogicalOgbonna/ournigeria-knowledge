# Adekunle Rufai Omoaje

> elected

## Biography

A grassroots politician and businessman from Ede, Osun State, recognised by INEC on its portal as Action Alliance National Chairman on 17 December 2025 following a Federal High Court (Osogbo) ruling. The chairmanship remains contested, with a rival Federal High Court (Abuja) ruling affirming Kenneth Udeze.
